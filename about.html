Discover Walton Hall & Hotel

The 16th Century Grade II listed, four-star Walton Hall and the modern Walton Hotel are nestled within 65 acres of glorious countryside, overlooking a serene lake. Located near Warwick and Stratford-upon-Avon, just 10 minutes from the M40 and close to Birmingham International Airport, our venue is ideal for both corporate and private events.

Enjoy the Orangery Restaurant, 240 free on-site parking spaces, a relaxing health & leisure club with pool, and high-speed fibre optic broadband across the estate.

Getting Here

By Road:
From the North (M40 Junction 13): Exit and turn left, then take the first right. Pass through Newbold Pacey and turn left at the T-junction in Wellesbourne. Continue past the Watermill and follow the single-lane road for 1.5 miles.

From the South (M40 Junction 15): Exit and take the fourth exit onto the A429 toward Cirencester. Drive through Barford, turn left at the roundabout to Wellesbourne, then turn right after the Watermill and continue for 1.5 miles along the single-lane road.
