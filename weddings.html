Craft Your Perfect Day

At Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa, your wedding day is crafted with timeless elegance. Nestled within 65 acres of breathtaking landscapes, our 16th-century Hall offers a romantic backdrop, complete with the historic St. James Chapel for your vows.

From the moment you arrive, our venue promises unparalleled photo opportunities. Choosing the perfect setting is a pivotal decision, and our dedicated team is here to orchestrate every detail, ensuring a seamless celebration tailored to your vision.

Whether envisioning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration with up to 150 guests, we cater to all love stories—marriages, civil partnerships, or vow renewals—delivering a day without compromise.

Unforgettable Celebrations

Every detail, from ceremony to reception, is crafted with care.

To ensure your dreams become a reality, our venue and team are dedicated to making your special day as perfect and individual as you are. There are endless possibilities when choosing our venue for your special day.

We will help you plan your perfect, bespoke wedding down to even the finest details. We will help you organise your wedding day with the minimum of fuss and maximum enjoyment, to ensure your wedding is just how you imagined it, from your wedding breakfast to your evening reception.

We offer tailor-made packages with attention to every detail, where you and your guests can enjoy a menu of seasonal, locally inspired flavours. Our Wedding Planner will get to know you and understand what matters most to you on your wedding day. You can relax knowing every item on your to-do list is taken care of.

We are also pleased to offer preferential accommodation rates for your guests, which can also be extended to prior and after your wedding for those guests wishing to take advantage of a longer stay and explore our surrounding historical towns.
