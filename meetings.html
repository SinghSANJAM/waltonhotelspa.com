Meetings @ Sunday Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

Nestled in 65 acres of exquisite Warwickshire countryside, Sunday Walton Hall Hotel & Spa is a premier destination for corporate and private events. The historic 16th-century Hall, once home to the Mordaunt family, combines timeless elegance with state-of-the-art facilities, creating an inspiring setting for your meetings.

Designed as a dedicated training, conference, and events venue, our contemporary hotel features purpose-built facilities ideal for board meetings, training sessions, and large-scale events. The self-contained Courtyard Suite accommodates up to 180 delegates theatre-style, perfect for product presentations and corporate gatherings.

Our 25 event spaces, including meeting rooms, restaurants, and outdoor areas, offer unmatched flexibility, set against a backdrop of Victorian gardens and manicured lawns. All rooms include complimentary WiFi, with our in-house AV team ready to support your technical needs.

Hotel Facilities at a Glance

  • 25 event spaces, including meeting rooms and restaurants, largest seating 220 theatre-style
  • 197 bedrooms, including 37 luxury rooms in our 16th Century Hall
  • Moncreiffe Restaurant, Mordaunt Restaurant, Orangery Restaurant
  • Conservatory Bar, Lemon Tree Bar, Hall Lounge
  • Spa & Leisure Club with indoor pool, gym, sauna, and beauty rooms
  • Exclusive use available
  • Complimentary WiFi throughout
  • Ample free on-site parking

Flexible Packages

Our bespoke day and 24-hour delegate packages are tailored to your meeting needs, ensuring a seamless and productive event. Packages include:

  • Complimentary standard WiFi (upgrades available)
  • Personal greeting from your dedicated meeting host
  • Fully set-up room with coffee or specialty teas on arrival
  • Unlimited refreshments throughout the day, served as you prefer
  • Optional coffee and snack to-go for delegates
  • Seasonal, healthy lunch options tailored to your requirements
  • LCD screens with user guides and on-site AV support
  • Transparent ‘one bill’ account with no hidden costs

Special Events

Elevate your private occasions with our exquisite venues. Host intimate private dinners in the Director Suite, celebrate proms with grandeur in the Moncreiffe Restaurant, or mark engagements with a bespoke event in the Courtyard Suite. Our team ensures every detail reflects your vision, from tailored menus to elegant décor.
